Add Food Flash to Home Screen
{% comment %}
  1. Open Safari: Make sure you're using Safari (Apple’s default browser). Other browsers like Chrome or Firefox won’t support this feature on iOS.
  2. Tap the Share icon: Look for the square with an upward arrow at the bottom of the screen.

    Example: Safari's Share Button
  3. Scroll Down and Tap “Add to Home Screen”:
    You’ll see the Food Flash icon and app name.
  4. Confirm by Tapping “Add”: Tap Add on the top-right corner. The Food Flash icon will now appear on your home screen like a regular app.Visit the web app now from the homescreen
